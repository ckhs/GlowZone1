What if the date I want is booked?

No worries, if your desired date is booked, simply call a Glow Zone representative at 1-800-435-0987. We will try our best to find and make any arrangements for your convinience.

Is Glow Zone open on holidays?

Of course we are! We value family time gatherings, so we are open to your personal events at any time of the year. Yes, even on Christmas.

We highly recommend that you book your event a week earlier just to lock in the date. Check our calendar for our available dates.
